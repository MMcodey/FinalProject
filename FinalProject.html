Rolled Icecream

Rolled ice cream is a type of frozen dessert that's made by spreading a
flavored milk-based liquid on a cold pan and then rolling it up into tight cylinders.
Which allows you to be able to mix in toppings/different base ice cream flavors
to create your own unique dessert

Locations
